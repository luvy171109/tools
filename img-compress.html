How to Compress Images

  1. Upload your image by dragging and dropping or clicking the upload area
  2. Adjust the compression level using the slider (higher = better quality)
  3. Select your preferred output format or leave it on "Auto"
  4. Click "Compress Image" and wait a few seconds
  5. Download your optimized image

Why Compress Images?

Image compression helps reduce file sizes while maintaining visual quality. This leads to:

  • Faster website loading times
  • Reduced bandwidth usage
  • Improved SEO rankings
  • Easier sharing via email or messaging apps
  • More storage space on your devices
